Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I drop off donations?

We have one drop-off location at our house: 28230 Green Forest Bluff Trl, Katy, TX 77494. Simply pack your donations securely in trash bags or similar containers and leave them on our front porch. View on Google Maps

Do you offer pickup services?

Yes! We offer pickup services for large donations in the Katy and Fulshear areas. Please contact us to schedule a convenient time. We'll need at least a week's notice to arrange pickup.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! Opportunities include sorting donations, organizing clothing drives, and assisting with distribution. Fill out the contact form above selecting "Volunteer opportunities" and we'll get back to you with current needs and scheduling.

I need clothing assistance. How do I get help?

We work in partnership with Family Hope to identify and serve families in need. Please contact us or reach out to Family Hope directly. All requests are handled with dignity and confidentiality.

Can my business or organization partner with you?

Absolutely! We partner with local businesses, schools, churches, and community organizations. You can host a collection box, organize a clothing drive, or support us in other ways. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

What items do you accept?

We accept gently used clothing for all ages, shoes, and accessories. All items should be clean and in good condition. Visit our Donate page for detailed guidelines on what we can accept.
