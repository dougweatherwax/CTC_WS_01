How Your Donations Help

Every piece of clothing you donate makes a real difference in someone's life. Your gently used items provide families with the clothing they need for work, school, and everyday life. Beyond meeting basic needs, your donations help restore dignity, build confidence, and support family stability throughout the Katy and Fulshear communities.

When you donate to Clothe to Care, you're not just giving away clothing‚Äîyou're investing in your community, supporting local families, and promoting sustainability by giving items a second life.

Preparing Your Donation

To make the donation process smooth and efficient, please:

  1. Verify adult sizes: Please ensure all items are adult clothing and shoes
  2. Check condition: Ensure all items meet our donation guidelines
  3. Wash and fold: Clean items should be neatly folded
  4. Pack securely: Use sturdy trash bags, garbage bags, or similar containers
  5. Tie bags carefully: Ensure bags are tightly sealed to protect items from weather
  6. Leave on front porch: Place bags on our front porch at a protected location if possible
