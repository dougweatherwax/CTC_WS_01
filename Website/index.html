Making a Difference, One Garment at a Time

Clothe to Care collects clothing donations from the Katy and Fulshear communities and distributes them to families in need through our partnership with Family Hope.

Donate Clothing Learn More

Our Mission

Clothe to Care is dedicated to providing clothing to families in need throughout the Katy and Fulshear, Texas areas. Through generous community donations and our partnership with Family Hope, we ensure that everyone has access to clean, quality clothing regardless of their circumstances.

How It Works

1

Collect

We collect gently used clothing donations from generous residents and businesses in Katy and Fulshear.

2

Sort & Organize

Our volunteers carefully sort and organize donations to ensure quality items reach those in need.

3

Distribute

Working with Family Hope, we distribute clothing to families throughout the community who need support.

Our Partnership with Family Hope

Clothe to Care is proud to partner with Family Hope, a local charity dedicated to supporting families in need throughout our community. This partnership allows us to identify families who would benefit from clothing donations and ensures our contributions make a meaningful impact.

Together, we're building a stronger, more compassionate community where everyone has access to the basic necessities they need to thrive.
