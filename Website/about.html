About Clothe to Care

Learn more about our mission, values, and the community we serve

Our Story

Clothe to Care was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure that every family in our community has access to clean, quality clothing regardless of their financial circumstances. What started as a small initiative among neighbors in Katy and Fulshear, Texas, has grown into a community-wide movement of compassion and generosity.

We recognized that clothing is not just a basic necessity—it's a fundamental aspect of dignity, self-confidence, and social participation. By connecting generous donors with families in need, we're building bridges of compassion throughout our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to collect, organize, and distribute quality clothing donations to families in need throughout the Katy and Fulshear areas. Through our partnership with Family Hope and the support of our generous community, we strive to:

  • Provide access to clean, quality clothing for all community members
  • Reduce textile waste by giving clothing a second life
  • Build connections between donors and recipients
  • Support family stability and dignity
  • Create a culture of generosity and mutual support in our community

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every family with empathy and understanding, recognizing that everyone deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.

Community

We believe in the power of neighbors helping neighbors. Our strength comes from the collective generosity of the Katy and Fulshear communities.

Stewardship

We honor our donors' contributions by carefully managing and distributing donations to maximize their impact on families in need.

Collaboration

Through our partnership with Family Hope, we ensure donations reach those who need them most while building a stronger support network.

Sustainability

By giving clothing a second life, we reduce waste and promote environmental responsibility while serving our community.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and accountability, ensuring every donation is handled with care and reaches those in need.

Partnership with Family Hope

Our collaboration with Family Hope is central to our mission. Family Hope is a respected local charity with deep roots in the Katy and Fulshear communities, dedicated to supporting families through various assistance programs.

Through this partnership, we benefit from Family Hope's established relationships with families in need, their expertise in community service, and their infrastructure for identifying and serving those who would benefit most from clothing donations. Together, we create a seamless system that connects generous donors with families who need support.

This collaboration allows us to focus on what we do best—collecting and organizing clothing donations—while ensuring our contributions reach the right families at the right time.

Serving Katy & Fulshear

Katy and Fulshear are vibrant, growing communities with strong values of neighborly support and mutual aid. Our organization is proud to be rooted in these communities, serving our neighbors and working to ensure that growth and prosperity are shared by all residents.

We accept donations from residents and businesses throughout the area and work to ensure that families in both communities have access to the clothing they need. Whether you're in Katy, Fulshear, or the surrounding areas, Clothe to Care is here to serve.
